# Company Profile: StreamLine SaaS

## Overview
- **Name**: StreamLine SaaS Inc.
- **Product**: StreamLine — a cloud-based project management and team collaboration platform
- **Founded**: 2022
- **Employees**: 45
- **Customers**: 2,500+ companies, 15,000+ active users
- **Pricing Tiers**: Starter ($19/mo), Professional ($49/mo), Enterprise ($149/mo)

## Product Features
- Task management with Kanban boards and Gantt charts
- Team chat and video conferencing
- File sharing and document collaboration
- Time tracking and reporting
- API integrations (Slack, GitHub, Jira, Google Workspace)
- Custom workflows and automation

## Support Structure
- **Support Email**: support@streamline-saas.com
- **Support Hours**: AI FTE handles 24/7; human team available 9 AM–6 PM EST M–F
- **SLA**: First response within 1 hour, resolution within 24 hours
- **Escalation Path**: AI FTE → Support Team Lead → Engineering → VP Product

## Common Customer Segments
- Small startups (1–10 users) — mostly Starter plan
- Mid-size companies (10–50 users) — Professional plan
- Enterprise (50–500 users) — Enterprise plan with dedicated support
